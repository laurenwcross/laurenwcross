About

Experience you can count on

Currently accepting new clients

I'm Lauren — a financial operations professional with over a decade of experience helping businesses keep their books clean, their accounts organized, and their operations running efficiently.

Alongside my financial background, I bring a strong legal assistant foundation with a focused emphasis on supporting technology companies and growing businesses. I have extensive experience drafting, reviewing, and organizing business agreements, ensuring they are clear, enforceable, and aligned with real-world operations. My work regularly includes SaaS contracts, software development agreements, service agreements, and intellectual property provisions.

My background also spans accounts receivable, financial systems management, and process optimization across multiple industries, including HVAC services. I take a detail-oriented, systems-driven approach to every engagement — whether helping a startup implement its first QuickBooks setup or assisting an established company with contract structuring and internal process improvements.

I bridge the gap between finance and legal operations, helping businesses — especially in the technology space — build strong foundations, reduce risk, and operate with confidence.
